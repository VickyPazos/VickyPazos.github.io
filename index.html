    Info

    Professional in UX/UI design qualified in Information and Documentation with extensive experience across multiple industries. I have an extensive academic grounding and additionally my experience has allowed me to gain analytical, organizational, collaborative and empathetic skills. Through my jobs, I have learned to develop myself, to listen and apply a utilitarian mindset to my work.

    In Design, I not only found my passion but also the perfect playground to combine my skills and abilities to achieve exceptional results!

    I have enjoyed graphic design since I was a child. I initially taught myself using online courses and resources and later leveraged my knowledge to get my first job. I discovered the world of UX/UI Design almost at the end of my degree, and since then, it has been my professional and academic passion.

    The following year, I combined an official master's degree at the UNIR with a UX/UI Design bootcamp in IronHack while continuing to work. This was a year of personal advancement and deep learning. I am interested in positions that would allow me to continue growing and learning as UX/UI Designer. I am also interested in connecting with other who share my passion.
